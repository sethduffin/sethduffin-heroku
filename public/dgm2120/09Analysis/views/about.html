About

Shoe Crew is designed to combine everyone’s favorite shoes into one location. Here you can find all the latest trends and styles organized by brand and collection. Shoe Crew makes it easy by redirecting you to the actual sellers with one click. Eventually, you will be able to save your own collections and share them with friends.

The owner, Seth Duffin, thought of the idea when he was having a hard time keeping track of when and where to find new shoe drops. Shoe crew takes care of this by providing that information all in one place. He hopes that others will take advantage of this tool and share with others the latest shoe finds!

Shoe Terminology

Term Definition
Aglet A metal or plastic tag at the end of a shoelace. The aglet makes lacing easier and it protects the lace from unraveling.
Ball The padded area of the foot between the big toe and the arch of the foot.
Back Seam The vertical seam at the center of the back of a shoe or boot.
Pitch The angle of the back part of the heel where it meets the sole, compared to the front part of the heel where it meets the sole. On a high-heeled shoe the pitch should be at a larger angle, in order to stabilize the heel.
Sole The entire part of the shoe that sits below the wearer’s foot. As opposed to the upper. The upper and sole make up the whole of the shoe.
